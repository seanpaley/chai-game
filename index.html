Sean has always loved chai, but never learned to make it properly.

One day, his colleague Pooja overhears him microwaving a tea bag with milk.

"Beta... what are you doing?! That is NOT chai!"

And so begins Sean's journey through Pooja's Chai Academy — 5 levels of increasingly complex recipes, judged by the most demanding chai critic in the office.

Tara will be there to ask the questions you're too afraid to ask.

How to Play

🧂 1. Select Ingredients

Pick the right ingredients from the shelf. Watch out for traps — Pooja will NOT be happy if you add lemon to chai!

👊 2. Prepare Spices

Some ingredients need special preparation. Crush, slice, grate, or leave whole — the method matters!

🔥 3. Cook

Control the heat and add ingredients in the right order. Water before tea, tea before milk!

⏱ 4. Time It

Pull the chai off the heat at the perfect moment. Too early = weak, too late = bitter.

☕ 5. Serve

Choose the right vessel and don't forget to strain!

⭐ Scoring

100 points total across 5 categories (20 each): Ingredients, Preparation, Order, Timing, and Presentation.

  • 0–49 = 0 stars
  • 50–69 = 1 star
  • 70–84 = 2 stars
  • 85–100 = 3 stars
